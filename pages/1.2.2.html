1.2.2 Identify the Problem - Quick Notes

Identifying the problem means establishing the consequence or impact and listing symptoms, which helps prioritize the case.

Gather Info from the User

  • What are the exact error messages?
  • Is anyone else having the same problem?
  • How long has this been happening?
  • What changed recently, either user-initiated or from another support request?
  • Did it work before? Were there any environmental or infrastructure changes?
  • Have they tried anything to fix it?

Backups

Check when the last backup was made and perform one before changing anything if possible.
