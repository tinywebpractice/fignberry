2.1.14 Molex Power Connectors - Notes

Molex is a legacy 4-pin power connector used to supply power from the PSU to older internal components. Modern devices usually use SATA power connectors instead.

Molex Wire Color Codes

Wire color Voltage Purpose
Yellow 12V DC Powers motors and drives
Red 5V DC Powers logic circuits
Black Ground Ground, with two pins used

Physical description: white or clear plastic housing with 4 pins total.

Key Things to Remember

  • Data cables carry data only, so power always needs a separate connector.
  • Newer devices use the SATA power connector with 15 pins.
  • Legacy devices use the Molex power connector with 4 pins.
  • Some devices include both SATA and Molex connectors.
  • DC means direct current, which is unidirectional electrical flow and what powers internal components.
