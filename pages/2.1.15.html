2.1.15 External SATA (eSATA) - Notes

eSATA is the external version of SATA and is designed for connecting external storage drives.

  • Maximum cable length is 2 meters, compared with 1 meter for internal SATA.
  • It requires a dedicated eSATA cable because internal SATA cables are not compatible.
  • eSATAp is a non-standard vendor variant that supports both USB and SATA through the same port by using an eSATAp cable.
  • Even though eSATA exists, USB remains the dominant standard for external drives.
