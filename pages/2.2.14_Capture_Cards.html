2.2.14 Capture Cards - Notes

A capture card is the opposite of a graphics card. Instead of generating video output, it records video input and either saves it as a file or streams it live.

Types of Capture Cards

Type What It Does Source
Game capture card Records or streams gameplay PC or game console via HDMI
HDMI capture card Records from any HDMI source Consoles, camcorders, security cameras
TV tuner card Receives and records broadcast TV Live TV signal

Installation Types

Type Connection Pros Best For
Internal PCIe slot Lower latency, higher performance Professional use
External USB or Thunderbolt Portable, easy to set up Casual users, multi-device use

Key Thing to Remember

Graphics card means video out to a monitor. Capture card means video in from a source.
